About

About

We are hardcore nerds for what we do. And we love it.

We think of us as artisans of the digital era. We love focusing on one or two projects at a time, taking care of the details and making every product special as it deserves.

We are not afraid to think out of the box and reinvent the wheel whenever we find a new approach would make a better experience.

Our mind is mobile-centered but our applications range from desktop, to TV, to touch-enabled screens and totems.

We are a crew of peers, mostly located in Italy (our headquarter is in Verona), but we cover different timezones as well, thanks to the two of us who are currently working remotely.

Our clients range from startups to fellow agencies and global brands.
